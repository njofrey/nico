Less is more

09 Jul 2025

After years of building products, I've landed on a simple truth: the best software does one thing, and does it exceptionally well.

More features create more clutter and more ways for things to break. A button you don't add is a decision you spare your user.

The real work isn't coding new options. It's having the discipline to refuse them. That's where you find focus and value.
