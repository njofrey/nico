Less is more

04 Jan 2025

After 7+ years building products across different markets, I've learned that the best software does one thing exceptionally well. Not ten things poorly.

Every feature you don't build is a feature that can't break. Every button you don't add is one less decision for your user. Every configuration option you skip is complexity avoided.

The hardest part isn't building features—it's saying no to them. But that's where the magic happens.
