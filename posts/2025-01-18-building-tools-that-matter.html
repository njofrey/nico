Building tools that matter

18 Jan 2025

Every tool I build starts with a real problem I've faced. Not market research, not competitor analysis—just genuine frustration with existing solutions that are bloated, slow, or overcomplicated.

The UF Calculator exists because checking Chile's daily UF value meant navigating cluttered financial portals. The VAT calculator exists because doing 19% math in my head got old fast. Simple problems, simple solutions.

The best tools feel invisible. They solve the problem so cleanly that you forget they exist—until you need them again.
