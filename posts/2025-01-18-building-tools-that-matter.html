Building tools that matter

16 Jul 2025

I build tools to solve my own frustrations with slow, bloated software.

The UF calculator came from my hassle with cluttered financial portals just to check one number. The VAT calculator exists to be faster than a spreadsheet for a repetitive task. I focus on problems I know.

The best tools blend into your workflow so smoothly you hardly notice them until they are gone yet you always know exactly where to find them when you need them.
