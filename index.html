Shipped Work

Radio DJ Batista Online

Problem: Accessing a live radio stream should be instant, not buried under slow-loading pages, ads, or complex interfaces.

Solution: A minimalist, single-purpose web player that streams Radio Batista live. It loads instantly and has one function: play.

Tech: Static HTML/JS with a persistent audio player, deployed on Vercel.

World Clock

Problem: Scheduling meetings across different time zones is complex and often leads to confusion and errors.

Solution: A minimalist world clock to see multiple time zones at a glance. Add cities to find the perfect meeting time without the mental math.

Tech: Static HTML/CSS/JS for instant load times.

UF Calculator

Problem: Finding the official UF value in Chile often means navigating slow, cluttered financial portals.

Solution: A clean, single-screen tool that provides the daily value and an instant calculator. No ads, no bloat.

Tech: Static HTML/CSS/JS for page loads under 100 ms.
