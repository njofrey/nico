Things I've Built

Messa Studio

Problem: Traditional floral design websites are often cluttered and don't showcase the transformative power of floral art in spaces.

Solution: A clean, portfolio-focused website that highlights how floral compositions transform atmospheres and create lasting memories. Each project tells a unique story.

Tech: Astro static site, elegant typography and minimal design.

Radio DJ Batista Online

Problem: Accessing a live radio stream should be instant, not buried under slow-loading pages, ads, or complex interfaces.

Solution: A minimalist, single-purpose web player that streams Radio Batista live. It loads instantly and has one function: play.

Tech: SonicPanel streaming, HTML/JS with persistent audio player.

World Clock

Problem: Scheduling meetings across different time zones is complex and often leads to confusion and errors.

Solution: A minimalist world clock to see multiple time zones at a glance. Add cities to find the perfect meeting time without the mental math.

Tech: Static HTML/CSS/JS for instant load times.

UF Calculator

Problem: Finding the official UF value in Chile often means navigating slow, cluttered financial portals.

Solution: A clean, single-screen tool that provides the daily value and an instant calculator. No ads, no bloat.

Tech: Static HTML/CSS/JS for page loads under 100 ms.

VAT 19% Calculator

Problem: Calculating the 19% VAT is a repetitive task for freelancers and small businesses that often leads to simple errors.

Solution: A straightforward tool designed to be quicker than opening a spreadsheet for the most common VAT calculation.

Tech: Static HTML/CSS/JS, deployed on Vercel.

CLP Exchange Dashboard

Problem: Financial websites are filled with noise when all you need is a quick view of the main currency exchange rates.

Solution: A minimalist dashboard showing the key currency pairs at a glance. No ads, no distractions, just the numbers.

Tech: Static HTML and lightweight JS.
